# Localization Glossary

| Term | Arabic Translation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Offer | عرض | Use consistently for artist offers.
| Proposal | عرض | Prefer “عرض” for any proposal copy.
| Order | طلب | Use when referencing customer requests.
| Delivery | تسليم | Use for final handoff messaging.
| Progress | التقدّم | Keep tone consistent; avoid synonyms.
| Artist | فنان | Lowercase inside sentences for consistency.
| Customer | عميل | Prefer this term for buyers.
